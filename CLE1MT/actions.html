Actions: What can YOU do?

Reduce Daily Waste

Helping the planet starts with what we buy and throw away. You can make a big difference just by cutting back on single-use plastics. Switch to a reusable water bottle, bring your own bags to the store, and try to reuse items instead of tossing them out. Less trash means cleaner oceans and less crowded landfills.

Save Energy and Water

Small changes at home can save a lot of resources. Try to turn off lights when you leave a room, unplug devices you aren't using, and fix leaky faucets quickly. You can also lower your carbon footprint by walking or biking for short trips, or by adding a few more plant-based meals to your weekly routine.

Supporting Ecosystems and Local Businesses

You can protect the environment right in your own neighborhood. Planting native flowers on a porch or yard gives local bees and butterflies a place to thrive. Additionally, buying local food and supporting eco-friendly shops helps reduce the pollution caused by shipping goods long distances.
