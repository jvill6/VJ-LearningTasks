We're Horizon Green, and we're a community driven organization which focuses on creating a more sustainable future for all, step by step.

Our Achievements:

1.5 Million Trees Planted

Through our tree planting initiatives, we've successfully planted over 1.5 million trees in deforested areas, contributing to carbon sequestration and biodiversity restoration.

500 Tons of Waste Recycled

Our recycling programs have diverted over 500 tons of waste from landfills, promoting a circular economy and reducing environmental pollution.

100 Community Workshops Conducted

We've conducted over 100 workshops in local communities, educating thousands of individuals on sustainable practices and environmental stewardship.
