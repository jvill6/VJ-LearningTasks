Introduction

We are Horizon Green. An organization focused on helping preserve the environment of The Earth. Our mission statement is to raise awareness on individual actions that affect our planet, whether they realize it or not. We are driven by the belief that a healthy planet is the foundation for all human progress, Horizon Green works at the intersection of community action, sustainable development, and ecological preservation. Our mission is to secure a vibrant, biodiverse, and resilient Earth for generations to come. By championing reforestation efforts, advocating for clean energy alternatives, and implementing local conservation projects, we are committed to turning environmental concern into measurable global impact.
